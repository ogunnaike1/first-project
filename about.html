We are a collective of designers, artists, chefs, entertainers, photographers, actors and writers.
We organise imaginative and engaging events, pop-ups and promotions: from idea generation through to production and management. We also collaborate with others and offer consultancy for ideas that are already in motion.
Our events aim to confuse and delight the senses within a unique and memorable atmosphere. Additionally we actively encourage and use young artists from all disciplines in order to give them early industry recognition.
We're available for corporate events, press and media launches, brand events, media mailers, product innovation, team building, festivals, weddings and of course, good ol' fashioned parties.
Making the impossible, possible
Working across the creative canvas, we make everything happen - from product design and small activation to full experiential events.
We have had the pleasure of working across the globe with leading food and beverage brands but have also kept things close to home in our London studios.
We offer full design production for brands including promotional merchandise design and manufacutre through to styling and photography. Your one-stop-shop for experiential creative design.

Brandy K
The most organised designer you could ever wish to meet, Brandy holds a BSc in Product Design and has worked freelance for clients including Lego and Bompas & Parr.In fact, this was where fate led her to meet Robin (she's also mad about food innovation and technology). A Canadian in accent but a true Brit at heart, Brandy can make a glow in the dark cocktail whilst balancing a tortoise on her head. No mean feat.
Robin Fegen
Robin has a BSc from UCL and a background in writing for children's television. After interning and working for food innovators Bompas & Parr (where he met Brandy) his passion for food and space, amongst many other things, found its true home in the formation of The Robin Collective where he could merge random themes to his heart's content.
