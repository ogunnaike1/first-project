CONTACT US

Food Futurologists
We are leaders in experimental food and flavour development and are proud members of The Experimental Food Society.
We are a collective of designers, artists, chefs, entertainers, photographers, actors and writers. We organise imaginative and engaging events, pop-ups and promotions: from idea generation through to production and management. We also collaborate with others and offer consultancy for ideas that are already in motion.
Our events aim to confuse and delight the senses within a unique and memorable atmosphere. Additionally we actively encourage and use young artists from all disciplines in order to give them early industry recognition.
We're available for corporate events, press and media launches, product innovation, team building, festivals, weddings and of course, good ol' fashioned parties.
